Quick Chicken and Stuffing Casserole

Ingredients

  • 3 cups prepared stuffing
  • 2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 (12 ounce) jar chicken gravy
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8×8-inch baking pan.
  2. Step 2: Mix stuffing, chicken, broccoli, chicken gravy, and half of the Cheddar cheese in a large bowl. Spread into the prepared baking pan; sprinkle with the remaining Cheddar cheese.
  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through and the Cheddar cheese is melted, about 20 – 25 minutes.
