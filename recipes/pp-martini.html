Pumpkin Pie Martini

Ingredients

  • 2 Parts Rum Chata
  • 1 Part Vanilla Vodka
  • Large Spoonful of canned pumpkin

Directions

  1. Add ingredients to a shaker over ice and shake.
  2. Strain into martini glass and sprinkle with Pumpkin Pie Spice

Comments

Don't forget the pumpkin!
