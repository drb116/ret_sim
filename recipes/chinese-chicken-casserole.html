Chinese Chicken Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked, diced chicken
  • 2 cups sliced celery
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 8 oz water chestnuts, drained
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • ¾ cup mayonnaise

Topping

  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup cornflake crumbs

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the chicken, celery, mushrooms, rice, water chestnuts, onion, lemon juice, cream of chicken soup, and mayonnaise until evenly combined.
  2. In a small bowl, stir together the melted butter and cornflake crumbs. Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the casserole.
  3. Bake at 350°F for 35–45 minutes, until hot and bubbly and the topping is golden.
