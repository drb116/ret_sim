Chicken & Wild Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cans cream of celery soup
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup chicken bouillon broth
  • 1 box Uncle Ben’s wild rice (with seasoning packet)
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (deboned), cut in large pieces

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 325°F. Grease a 13×9-inch baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the cream soups, chicken bouillon broth, wild rice (with seasoning), and the uncooked white rice. Do not pre-cook the rice.
  3. Pour the mixture into the prepared pan.
  4. Place the pieces of deboned chicken breasts on top of the rice/soup mixture.
  5. Bake uncovered for about 2 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and the rice is tender.
  6. Make-ahead note: You can assemble this the night before. Cover and refrigerate; bake uncovered the next day (add a few extra minutes if starting cold).
