Grandma’s Hamburger Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • ¼ cup red wine
  • 2 (15-oz) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp white sugar (or to taste)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (or to taste)
  • 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1 tbsp basil
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 2 tsp parsley
  • 10-12 oz egg noodles
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese (or more to taste)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir the ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly (about 5 – 7 minutes); drain and discard the grease.
  2. Add red wine and reduce to about half.
  3. Mix in the tomato sauce, sugar, garlic, salt and other spices into the ground beef; simmer until flavors blend (about 20 minutes). Remove from heat and cover the skillet.
  4. While simmering, mix together the sour cream, cream cheese, and diced onion in a bowl.
  5. While the sauce is cooking, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook the egg noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but still firm to the bite (about 7 – 9 minutes). Drain.
  6. Layer ½ of the cooked egg noodles into the prepared casserole dish. Top with ½ of the sour cream mixture, then ½ of the ground beef mixture. Repeat layers once more. Sprinkle the shredded sharp Cheddar cheese over the top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven at 350 °F until the cheese is melted and golden brown (about 25 – 30 minutes).

Comments

I usually add a little sauce to the bottom, then a layer of noodles, then sauce, cheese/sour cream, layer of noodles, then sauce and shredded cheese.
