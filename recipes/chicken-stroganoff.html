Creamy Chicken and Mushroom Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 (12 ounce) package egg noodles
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Melt butter in a heavy skillet over medium heat; stir in onion and mushrooms. Reduce heat and cook until onions release their liquid and mushrooms soften, about 5 minutes.
  2. Step 2: Season chicken strips with Greek seasoning and black pepper. Add to the skillet and cook until lightly browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook noodles until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 minutes. Drain.
  4. Step 4: Pour chicken broth, milk, and Worcestershire sauce into the skillet. Slowly stir in sour cream, one tablespoon at a time. Spoon out a small amount of sauce and whisk with flour in a bowl until smooth. Stir the flour mixture back into the sauce; simmer until thickened, about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Serve over egg noodles and garnish with parsley.
