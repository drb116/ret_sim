Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
  • ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 carrots, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef or lamb
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • ¾ cup beef broth
  • ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and mash. Mix in butter, finely chopped onion, and ¼ cup shredded cheese. Season with salt and pepper; set aside.
  3. Bring another pot of salted water to a boil. Add carrots and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain and set aside. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Heat oil in a large frying pan. Add onion and cook until clear. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain fat. Stir in flour and cook 1 minute. Add ketchup and beef broth. Simmer 5 minutes.
  5. Spread ground beef in the bottom of a 2-quart casserole dish. Add mashed carrots, then mashed potatoes. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes, or until golden brown. Serve hot and enjoy!
