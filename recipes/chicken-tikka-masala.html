Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or more to taste (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 (14 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar, or more to taste (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces
  • ½ teaspoon curry powder

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Heat ghee in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add cumin, salt, ginger, cayenne pepper, cinnamon, and turmeric. Fry until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Stir in tomato sauce; bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  5. Step 5: Simmer for 10 minutes, then stir in cream, sugar, and paprika. Bring sauce to a simmer and cook, stirring often, until thickened, 10 to 15 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Heat vegetable oil in a separate skillet over medium heat. Stir in chicken and curry powder; sear until lightly browned but still pink inside, about 3 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Transfer chicken and pan juices into the sauce. Simmer until chicken is cooked through, about 30 minutes. Adjust salt and sugar to taste.

Comments

Good recipe, but budget about an hour of cooking.
